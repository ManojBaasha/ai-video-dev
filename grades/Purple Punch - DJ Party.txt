Purple Punch - DJ / Party / Club
==================================
Best for: Indoor parties, DJ sets, club scenes, laser/light shows, purple/pink ambient lighting

FFmpeg filter chain:
curves=r='0/0 0.1/0.03 0.5/0.48 0.85/0.9 1/1':g='0/0 0.1/0.02 0.5/0.42 0.85/0.84 1/1':b='0/0 0.1/0.04 0.5/0.52 0.85/0.92 1/1':m='0/0 0.08/0.02 0.5/0.52 0.85/0.94 1/1',colorbalance=rs=0.04:gs=-0.04:bs=0.06:rm=0.03:gm=-0.03:bm=0.04:rh=0.02:gh=-0.02:bh=0.03,eq=contrast=1.35:brightness=0.04:saturation=1.4,unsharp=5:5:0.6

What it does:
- Curves: Green channel suppressed (0.84 max) for purple/magenta lean, blue lifted
- Color balance: Red+blue pushed (=purple), green pulled across all tones
- EQ: Strong contrast to separate people from haze, high saturation for vivid lights
- Unsharp: Good sharpening to cut through smoke/haze

Notes:
- Works with purple, pink, and blue ambient lighting
- The green suppression is what makes it purple rather than blue
- For redder club lighting, increase rs/rm values and decrease bs/bm slightly
- Smoke/haze machines add atmosphere — the contrast boost helps cut through it
