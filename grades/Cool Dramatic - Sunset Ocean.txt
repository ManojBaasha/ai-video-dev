Cool Dramatic - Sunset / Ocean Cliff
======================================
Best for: Sunset scenes, ocean overlooks, cliff views, golden hour landscapes where you want cool blue tones

FFmpeg filter chain:
curves=r='0/0 0.05/0.05 0.12/0.1 0.25/0.22 0.5/0.44 0.7/0.66 0.85/0.84 1/0.95':g='0/0 0.05/0.06 0.12/0.12 0.25/0.24 0.5/0.48 0.7/0.7 0.85/0.88 1/0.97':b='0/0 0.05/0.06 0.12/0.12 0.25/0.26 0.5/0.52 0.7/0.76 0.85/0.93 1/1':m='0/0 0.04/0.04 0.1/0.1 0.5/0.54 0.85/0.94 1/1',colorbalance=rs=-0.06:gs=-0.03:bs=0.1:rm=-0.05:gm=-0.02:bm=0.06:rh=-0.01:gh=0.0:bh=0.02,eq=contrast=1.2:brightness=0.08:saturation=1.3,unsharp=5:5:0.5

What it does:
- Curves: Many control points for smooth tonal transitions, red capped, blue fully open
- Shadows are LIFTED (not crushed) — you can see grass, rocks, people on cliffs
- Color balance: Strong blue push in shadows/mids
- EQ: Lower contrast (1.2) and higher brightness (0.08) to keep shadow detail
- Saturation 1.3 brings out greens in vegetation

Notes:
- This grade keeps shadow detail visible — grass, rocks, people won't become silhouettes
- If you want more dramatic/crushed shadows, reduce brightness to 0.01 and increase contrast to 1.4
- The sun/reflection naturally stays warm because it's pure white/overexposed — the blue only affects mids and shadows
